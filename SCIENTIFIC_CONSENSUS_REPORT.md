### 2. Confidence Score Metrics (0-100)
For each theory, calculated confidence based on:
- **Empirical Support** (0-100): Quality and quantity of experimental evidence
- **Replication Rate** (0-100): Percentage of findings successfully replicated
- **Citation Impact** (0-100): H-index and citation count relative to field
- **Academic Acceptance** (0-100): Percentage of researchers who accept theory
- **Predictive Power** (0-100): Ability to make novel, testable predictions
- **Falsifiability** (0-100): Clarity of conditions that would refute theory

### 3. Consensus Narratives
Each theory includes:
- Evidence-based status explanation
- Supporting community (disciplines and researchers)
- Major critics and their objections
- Current trend (rising, stable, declining, reviving)
- Year established and peak influence year
- Key milestones with impact assessment

### 4. Interactive Visualization
- **Comparison Table**: Sort by confidence score or status
- **By Status View**: Group theories by consensus category
- **Theory Detail View**: Deep dive into any theory with:
  - Confidence score and interpretation
  - Detailed metrics breakdown
  - Consensus narrative
  - Supporting community
  - Major critics
  - Historical milestones
